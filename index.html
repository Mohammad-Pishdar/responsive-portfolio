About Me

I am an aspiring web-developer who has recently completed the Coding Boot Camp as a collaboration between the University of Sydney and Trilogy Education Services as I was seeking a career change into the tech sector. I believe the skills that I have learned during the boot camp have completely enabled me to take on a challenge of working as a full-stack web developer. Since the start of my studies, I have dedicated all my free time to practice coding and to learn new web dev skills as they have been presented to us by our mentors. I hope I can continue being a learner of this amazing field of knowledge and hopefully someday contribute educating other newbies like myself to learn such life-changing skill.
